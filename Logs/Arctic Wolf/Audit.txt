sw - starts with
eq - equal
pr - present (has value)

Login / authentication activity
actor.alternateId eq "user@domain.com" and eventType sw "user.authentication."

MFA Bypass
eventType eq "user.mfa.attempt_bypass"

All MFA activity for the user
actor.alternateId eq "user@domain.com" and eventType eq "user.authentication.auth_via_mfa"


Okta Verify push only
actor.alternateId eq "user@domain.com" and eventType eq "user.authentication.auth_via_mfa" and debugContext.debugData.factor eq "OKTA_VERIFY_PUSH"
or 
actor.alternateId eq "user@domain.com" and eventType eq "user.authentication.auth_via_mfa" and debugContext.debugData.factorType eq "push"


Search suspicious MFA outcomes 
actor.alternateId eq "user@domain.com" and eventType eq "user.authentication.auth_via_mfa" and (outcome.result eq "UNANSWERED" or outcome.result eq "ABANDONED" or outcome.result eq "FAILURE")

Search for same source IP across users
client.ipAddress eq "x.x.x.x"

Same IP with failures only
client.ipAddress eq "x.x.x.x" and outcome.result eq "FAILURE"

Session termination / logout
actor.alternateId eq "user@domain.com" and eventType sw "user.session."

Sign-on policy decisions
actor.alternateId eq "user@domain.com" and eventType eq "policy.evaluate_sign_on"

Suspicious activity events
eventType sw "user.account.report_suspicious_activity"
